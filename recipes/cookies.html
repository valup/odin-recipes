Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These classic chocolate chip cookies are soft, chewy, and loaded with chocolate chips. Perfect for any occasion!

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup white sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 2 cups semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 165°C (325°F).
  2. Grease cookie sheets or line with parchment paper.
  3. In a bowl, sift together the flour, baking soda, and salt. Set aside.
  4. In another bowl, beat the melted butter with white and brown sugar until smooth.
  5. Beat in the egg, egg yolk, and vanilla extract to the butter mixture until light and creamy.
  6. Gradually add the dry ingredients to the wet ingredients and stir until combined.
  7. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  8. Drop spoonfuls of dough 3 inches apart onto the baking sheets.
  9. Bake for 15-17 minutes or until edges are lightly golden.
  10. Let cool on the baking sheet for a few minutes before transferring to a wire rack.
