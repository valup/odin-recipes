Oat Chocolates

Description

A classic argentinian recipe, very simple to make and extremely delicious.

Ingredients

  • 200grs melted butter
  • 250grs plain oats
  • 200grs bitter cocoa powder
  • 4 to 5 tablespoons dulce de leche
  • Shredded coconut (optional)
  • Chocolate liners

Steps

  1. In a bowl, add the melted butter, oats and bitter cocoa powder.
  2. Mix together with a spatula or wooden spoon.
  3. Incorporate the dulce de leche and finish mixing everything together.
  4. Bring the mix to the fridge.
  5. When the consistency is solid enough, remove from the fridge.
  6. With a tablespoon, take small portions of the mix and shape them into balls.
  7. Roll the balls in shredded coconut. If you don't like coconut, you can use sprinkles or melted chocolate.
  8. Place the balls into the chocolate liners.
